Inclement Weather Policy

    If Bethel Schools are closed:*
  • Kids Club will be Closed.
  • There will be no morning Group Exercise Classes.
  • Group Exercise classes will start at 4:30pm depending on the weather.
  • Sportsplex will be open depending on the weather.
    If Bethel Schools have a delayed opening:*
  • Group Exercise classes will start at 9:15am and will follow the normal schedule for the day.
  • Sportsplex will be open.
    If Bethel Schools have an early dismissal:*
  • Group Exercise classes will start at 6:00am as usual and will go to 12:15pm.
  • Sportsplex will be open in the morning and may stay open.
  • Group Exercise classes starting at 4:30pm will be help depending on the weather.
