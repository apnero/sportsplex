Vinyasa Yoga

Vinyasa is a term that covers a broad range of yoga classes. The word Vinyasa means “breath-synchronized movement”. The teacher will instruct you to move from one pose to the next on an inhale or an exhale. These fluid movements result in the poses flowing together like a dance.
